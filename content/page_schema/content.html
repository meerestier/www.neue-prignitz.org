Efficacy Assessment and Pharmacokinetics

Find the suitable model

Often it is difficult to find a suitable model for the preclinical efficacy test. We do not have answers for all cases, but since we are experienced in cardiovascular, inflammatory, orthopedic, hematological and oncological indications in various species, we are a good counterpart to find and establish or develop the suitable test method for your product. With our surgical unit we are prepared for surgical induced models, implantation of medical devices, as well as catheters for specific PK routes of administration and sampling. If you have a certain request, don't hesitate to contact us.
