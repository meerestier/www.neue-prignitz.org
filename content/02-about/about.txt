#### Vorteile für Besucher 
In Lower Saxony is our experimental farm preclinics research farm. The focus of this site is the job immunization to produce antibodies and antibody sera. A special feature of our offer, there is the immunization of camelids such as llamas and alpacas. In addition to abundant pasture land for the keeping of llamas, alpacas, goats and sheep there, we have also air-conditioned stables for the experimental approach. There we have to breed a "laboratory breed" started, which by smallness, asaisonale throw cycles and Wolllosigkeit (hair sheep) is ideal for research and science.
